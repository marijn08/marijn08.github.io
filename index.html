1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All data you enter into Macro Meter — including meals, food items, nutritional targets, and preferences — is stored locally on your device only using AsyncStorage. No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

✅

Your nutrition data never leaves your phone. We have zero access to it.

📷

2. Camera & Barcode Scanning

The App uses your device camera solely to scan food barcodes. Camera access is requested only when you choose to use the barcode scanner.

Images and scan data are processed entirely on-device and are never uploaded, stored remotely, or shared with anyone.

🔗

3. Third-Party Services & Advertising

Macro Meter may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These services may collect non-personal, anonymized data such as device type, general location (country/region), and ad interaction data to serve relevant ads. This data is handled according to each provider's own privacy policy.

We do not sell your personal information. No nutrition data, meal logs, or health-related information you enter in the App is ever shared with advertisers or any third party.

💾

4. Data Storage & Security

All your data resides exclusively on your device. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

We have no ability to access, recover, or view your data at any time.

👶

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected whatsoever, no special provisions are required.

📝

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
